**Date:** 2026-03-30

## Executive Summary

TAM/SAM/SOM analysis indicates a viable market opportunity above the $10M threshold. The total addressable market for drill-compatible cleaning accessories in the US is estimated at **$1.8B**. The serviceable addressable market for waterproof drill cleaning accessories specifically is estimated at **$320M**. The serviceable obtainable market over a 5-year horizon is estimated at **$28M–$52M**, well above the $10M minimum viability threshold. Market opportunity is real and defensible.

## 1. Methodology

This analysis uses a bottom-up market sizing approach, building from:
1. US household and commercial drill ownership data
2. Cleaning product and attachment purchasing behavior (survey data)
3. Competitive revenue benchmarks
4. Purchase intent from user validation study (63 survey respondents)

Cross-checked against top-down industry data from:
- IBISWorld: "Power Tool Accessories" industry report (2025)
- Statista: US home improvement and cleaning tool market data
- Amazon Best Seller rank benchmarking for drill cleaning attachment category

## 6. Growth Projections and Drivers

### 6.1 Market Tailwinds

- **Aging housing stock**: Older homes require more intensive grout/tile maintenance
- **Labor cost pressure**: Professional cleaners and property managers face rising wages, increasing demand for labor-saving tools
- **DIY culture growth**: Pandemic-era DIY habit formation has persisted; home improvement spend remains elevated
- **Drill attachment category growth**: Non-waterproof drill cleaning attachments grew ~22% YoY on Amazon 2023–2025 (BSR trend analysis), validating category momentum

### 6.2 Market Risks

- **Competitor response**: If waterproof drill housing proves successful, Drill Brush brand (well-capitalized, category leader) could launch competing product within 18–24 months
- **Price sensitivity**: Survey data shows purchase intent drops ~25% above $100; pricing discipline required
- **Consumer education burden**: Mainstream consumers may not self-identify as needing this product without category education
