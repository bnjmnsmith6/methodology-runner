## Compliance Matrix

### Material 1: Platinum-Cured Silicone (VMQ)

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Material** | Platinum-cured silicone rubber (food grade) |
| **CFR Section** | 21 CFR §177.2600 — Rubber articles intended for repeated use |
| **Compliance Status** | COMPLIANT |
| **Key Requirements Met** | (a) Consists of basic rubber components listed in §177.2600(b); (b) optional adjuvants listed in §177.2600(c); (c) no migration exceeding FDA threshold of regulation |
| **Restrictions** | Must be platinum-cured (not peroxide-cured) to avoid residual initiator migration; supplier must provide Certificate of Compliance (CoC) citing §177.2600 |
| **Required Documentation** | Supplier CoC citing 21 CFR §177.2600; extractables/leachables test report; migration test per FDA guidance (total extractables <1 mg/cm²) |
| **Heat Resistance Compliance** | Continuous 180°F — PASS (rated to 450°F) |
| **Dishwasher Compliance** | 140–160°F wash — PASS |
| **Notes** | Preferred grip/insulation material; widely used in FDA-compliant kitchenware; NSF/ANSI 51 certification also recommended |

### Material 2: PEEK (Polyether Ether Ketone)

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Material** | PEEK — Polyarylether ketone resin |
| **CFR Section** | 21 CFR §177.2415 — Polyarylether ketone resins |
| **Compliance Status** | COMPLIANT |
| **Key Requirements Met** | Resin must be manufactured from monomers listed in §177.2415(b); finished article must meet extractable limits per §177.2415(c) |
| **Restrictions** | Only grades manufactured per §177.2415 specifications are compliant; must verify specific commercial grade (e.g., Victrex 450G, Solvay KetaSpire KT-820) against CFR listing |
| **Required Documentation** | Supplier CoC citing 21 CFR §177.2415; FDA food contact notification (FCN) reference if applicable; extractables test data |
| **Heat Resistance Compliance** | Continuous 180°F — PASS (Tg 289°F; rated to 480°F continuous) |
| **Dishwasher Compliance** | 140–160°F wash — PASS |
| **Notes** | Excellent structural candidate; inert at coffee temperatures; confirm specific commercial grade compliance |

---

### Material 3: Stainless Steel 316

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Material** | Stainless Steel 316 (UNS S31600) |
| **CFR Section** | Not listed in 21 CFR Part 177 (Part 177 is for polymers only); metals regulated under general food contact principles |
| **Compliance Status** | COMPLIANT (via NSF/ANSI 51 and FDA GRAS principles) |
| **Key Requirements Met** | FDA evaluates metals under general safety (GRAS) framework per 21 CFR §170.3; NSF/ANSI 51 Standard for Food Equipment Materials covers 316 SS; USDA and FDA both accept 316 SS for food contact |
| **Restrictions** | Must meet ASTM A276 or A240 for alloy composition; welds must be passivated per ASTM A967 or A380; no free iron contamination |
| **Required Documentation** | Mill certification to ASTM A276/A240; NSF/ANSI 51 certification or equivalent third-party food contact approval; passivation certificate if welded |
| **Heat Resistance Compliance** | Continuous 180°F — PASS (rated to 1,650°F) |
| **Dishwasher Compliance** | Yes — PASS (316 resistant to chloride in dishwasher detergents) |
| **Notes** | If used as structural frame, requires silicone overmold/grip to prevent thermal burn to user; 316 preferred over 304 for chloride-environment dishwasher use |

---

### Material 4: PEI — Polyetherimide (Ultem)

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Material** | PEI — Polyetherimide resin (e.g., Sabic Ultem 1010) |
| **CFR Section** | 21 CFR §177.1630 — Polyimide resins |
| **Compliance Status** | CONDITIONALLY COMPLIANT |
| **Key Requirements Met** | PEI can be manufactured per §177.1630 specifications; Ultem 1010 is specifically formulated for food contact compliance |
| **Restrictions** | Not all PEI grades are food contact approved — must specify Ultem 1010 (or equivalent food-contact grade); standard Ultem 1000 is NOT listed; verify grade-specific CoC |
| **Required Documentation** | Grade-specific supplier CoC citing 21 CFR §177.1630; extractables/leachables data for Ultem 1010; compliance letter from Sabic or equivalent |
| **Heat Resistance Compliance** | Continuous 180°F — PASS (Tg 419°F; continuous service to 338°F) |
| **Dishwasher Compliance** | 140–160°F wash — PASS |
| **Notes** | Backup structural material; amber color typical; higher cost than PP/HDPE but substantially better thermal performance; confirm Ultem 1010 specifically |

## Non-Compliant / Excluded Materials

| Material | CFR Reference | Status | Reason for Exclusion |
|---|---|---|---|
| Peroxide-cured silicone | §177.2600 | CONDITIONAL FAIL | Residual peroxide by-products; post-cure required and difficult to verify in supply chain; not recommended when platinum-cured alternative available |
| PP (food grade) | §177.1520 | EXCLUDED (performance) | FDA-compliant but marginal thermal performance at 180°F continuous; creep risk under sustained load |
| HDPE (food grade) | §177.1520 | EXCLUDED (performance) | FDA-compliant but may soften/warp at 180°F; not suitable for structural use in this application |
| Aluminum (anodized) | GRAS (conditional) | EXCLUDED (documentation burden) | Bare aluminum reacts with acidic beverages; anodize layer FDA compliance difficult to certify; prefer SS316 for metal alternative |
