## 3. Market Gap Analysis

### Primary Gap
No product exists that:
1. Protects an **existing consumer drill** (corded or battery) from water ingress during cleaning
2. Maintains full **drill functionality** (speed control, torque)
3. Provides a standard interface for **cleaning brush attachments**
4. Is priced in the **$50–$120 consumer range**

### Secondary Gaps
- **Retrofit vs. purpose-built**: All waterproof drill solutions require purchasing a new drill. No retrofit housing exists at IPX5+.
- **Cleaning-specific design**: Waterproof drills (Makita, Milwaukee) are designed for construction/plumbing, not cleaning tasks. Ergonomics and attachment systems are not optimized for scrubbing.
- **Battery compatibility**: AquaShield (corded only) and HydroGrip (poor seals) leave battery drill owners with no viable option.
